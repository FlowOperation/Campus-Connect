# Moderation Guidelines

## Principles
- Apply policy consistently and transparently.
- Minimize harm while preserving legitimate discussion.
- Keep decisions reviewable and reversible.

## Moderator Workflow
1. Triage queue item by severity.
2. Review evidence snapshot and context.
3. Select action mapped to policy code.
4. Document rationale and duration.
5. Notify affected users when policy requires.

## Enforcement Policy Levels
- Level 0: no violation, dismiss report.
- Level 1: minor violation, warning.
- Level 2: significant violation, content removal and temporary restriction.
- Level 3: repeated or severe abuse, suspension or ban.

## Appeal Handling
- Appeals can be filed within defined window.
- Senior moderators review appeals.
- Final outcomes logged with full audit metadata.

## KPI Targets
- Median triage time < 2 hours.
- High-severity action time < 30 minutes.
- Appeal reversal rate monitored for policy quality.
